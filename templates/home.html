About Us

Bal Sakha Play School is located at Pul Pehladpur, Badarpur, New Delhi. We focus on early childhood education through activity-based learning and one-on-one interaction.

With a maximum strength of 30 students and two dedicated teachers, every child receives personal attention and guidance.
